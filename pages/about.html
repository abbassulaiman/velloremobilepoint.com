Our Journey

15 Years of Mobile Excellence in Vellore

From a humble mobile accessories stall to Vellore's most recognised mobile shop - the journey of Vellore Mobile Point is built on trust, expertise, and a genuine passion for technology.

2012 - The Beginning

Started operations in Vellore under a different name, selling mobile accessories and providing basic phone repair services. Our commitment to quality and honesty quickly built a loyal local customer base.

2015 - Expanded to Full Mobile Sales

Extended to full smartphone sales - covering Samsung, Redmi, Vivo, Realme, and more. Became a one-stop destination for mobile needs in Sathuvachari, Vellore.

2019 - Advanced Repair Centre

Upgraded to a professional repair centre offering same-day screen replacement, battery replacement, motherboard-level repairs, and water damage service for all major brands including iPhone and Samsung.

2024 - Rebranded as Vellore Mobile Point (VMP)

Officially rebranded as Vellore Mobile Point with a bold new identity to reflect our growth and vision. Launched social media presence and expanded our digital reach across Tamil Nadu.

2026 - Registered as LLP & VMP Brand Launch

Officially registered as Vellore Mobile Point LLP (LLPIN: ACY-3014) with ROC Chennai. Preparing to launch our own VMP Brand - a line of quality electronic accessories manufactured and branded exclusively by us.
